Privacy Policy

Celestial Oracle — Daily Astrology  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Celestial Oracle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Celestial Oracle stores the following information locally on your device only, using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API:

  • Your date of birth — used solely to calculate your Western and Chinese zodiac signs and personalise daily readings.
  • Your preferred notification time — used solely to schedule your optional daily cosmic reminder.

WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location or IP address data
  • No browsing history or web activity
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No financial or payment information
  • No data of any kind is sent to external servers

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Celestial Oracle loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) for display purposes only. This is a standard web font request and does not involve any personal data from our extension. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

DATA STORAGE

Your birth date and settings are stored via chrome.storage.sync, which may sync across your Chrome-signed devices. This sync is managed entirely by Google Chrome and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Takumi Studio has no access to this data.

DATA DELETION

You can delete all stored data at any time by opening the extension popup, navigating to the Settings tab, and selecting "Reset & Clear All Data". Uninstalling the extension also removes all locally stored data.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Celestial Oracle is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

CONTACT

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out to Takumi Studio via the Chrome Web Store listing page.
